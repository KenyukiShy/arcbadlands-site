Channel Priority Tiers
Tier 1
They come to you. Photographer/appraiser dispatched to your location at no charge. Highest leverage — start here.
Tier 2
Fee or transport required. They dispatch with a charge, or they'll appraise if you bring the vehicle to them. Work in parallel with Tier 1.
Tier 3
You do all the work. Self-submit, auction lotting, or direct sale where you coordinate photography, transport, and listing. Last resort or parallel fallback.
Channel Types
Consignment
Dealer lists your vehicle on consignment. You retain ownership until sale. Best for maximizing net return.
Auction
Hard cash close at live or online auction. Fastest liquidity but lowest ceiling. BaT (Bring a Trailer) is a premium online auction with collector buyers.
Direct Sale
Dealer or private buyer purchases outright. Fastest transaction but typically a wholesale floor. Use as a floor number, not a target.
BaT Proxy
Dealer manages your Bring a Trailer listing, photography, write-up, and Q&A. You get BaT exposure without self-submitting. Usually bundled with consignment.
Self-Submit
You build the listing yourself (BaT, RV Trader, etc.). Full control but 100% on you for photos, copy, Q&A, and logistics.
